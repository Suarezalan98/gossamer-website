About Me

I am a photographer, director, and videographer based in San Diego, CA. Growing up I always knew that I had an eye for photograph and a desire for perfection in the art of filmmaking and content creation. While growing up in the face of adversity has influenced my grungey, gritty, and dark style; I also focus on a clean minimalistic look that is pleasing to the eye.

Through living in San Diego, I ahve been able to gain expertise in the car, sports, fasion, bodybuilding, and nightlife scenes while perfecting the art of showcasing the beauty of the finer things in life that California has to offer.
